Operational Manual

This documentation outlines the standardized operating procedures (SOPs) for U-FIRST programs. All team members and partners are expected to adhere to these guidelines to ensure consistency and efficiency in our service delivery.

Service Protocols

Our engagement model follows a three-step process to ensure that our assistance is effective and measurable:

1. Assessment (Needs analysis)
2. Deployment (Resource allocation)
3. Review (Impact validation)

Data Policy

Transparency is our priority. All beneficiary data is processed securely and is subject to our internal audit protocols. We maintain a strict "no-leak" policy regarding private donor and beneficiary information.
